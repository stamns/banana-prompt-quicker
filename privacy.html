Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-21

Data Collection and Usage

Banana Prompt Quicker is committed to protecting your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:

  • We only access webpage content on Google AI Studio and Gemini to insert the prompt selector interface
  • All processing is done locally in your browser
  • Your favorited prompts and preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • We do not collect any personal identification information
  • We do not track your browsing history or behavior
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your AI conversations or inputs

Required Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your favorited prompts and custom prompts locally in your browser
  • Host Permissions (raw.githubusercontent.com): To fetch the latest prompt library updates from our GitHub repository
  • Content Scripts (aistudio.google.com, gemini.google.com): To inject the banana button and prompt selection interface on these pages

Data Sharing

We maintain strict data privacy:

  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties
  • We do not share your favorited prompts or custom prompts with anyone
  • Prompt library updates are fetched from public GitHub repositories
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally:

  • Your favorited prompts are saved in your browser's local storage using Chrome Storage API
  • Custom prompts you create are stored locally in your browser
  • No data is uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers
  • You can clear all stored data by removing the extension

Remote Code

This extension does not use remote code:

  • All JavaScript code is included in the extension package
  • We only fetch JSON data (prompt library) from GitHub, not executable code
  • No eval() or similar dynamic code execution is used

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can view, add, or remove favorited prompts at any time
  • You can create and delete custom prompts at any time
  • You can disable the extension temporarily or remove it completely
  • Removing the extension will delete all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension is designed for general productivity use and does not target children.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
